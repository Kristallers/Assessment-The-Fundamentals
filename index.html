An unofficial guide to Berkserk.

History of Berserk

Berserk (ベルセルク in Japanese) was originally premiered in 1988, but not officially published until 1989. Since then it has been an ongoing story. There are several movie and anime adaptations of the series.

Read more about Berserk

Kentaro Miura

Kentaro Miura, 三浦 建太郎, is the original author of Berserk. He passed away in May 2021, while the Manga was still ongoing.

Kouji Mori

Berserk is since 2022 continued by Miura's long time friend Kouji Mori, who is also a manga author.
